Known Limitations

Hard stops, workarounds, and fix timelines — review before scoping

ReferencePre-Sales
🚨 Scope Review Required

Review every limitation in this table with the customer before scoping the engagement — not after. Hard-stop limitations (High severity with no workaround) must be resolved before proceeding to a Statement of Work.

Exclusion Checklist

Before scoping IFP v2.1, confirm the customer does NOT have any of the following hard stops:

  • Requires BYOK encryption on public cloud infrastructure
  • Cannot use AWS-hosted services (public sector, federal, sovereign cloud)
  • Requires more than 8 dimensions in any planning model
  • Requires employee-level CapEx or headcount planning without OWP
  • Requires waterfall or reciprocal cost allocation out of box

If any box cannot be unchecked, stop and engage the Apps team before proceeding.
