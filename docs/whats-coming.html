What's Coming

Roadmap snapshot — GA items, coming soon, and ecosystem integrations

ReferenceSnapshot: March 2026
📅 Frozen Snapshot

Snapshot as of: March 2026 workshop session. Roadmap dates shift. Confirm all timelines with the Apps team at financeapplications@anaplan.com before quoting to a prospect.

⚠ Quoting Roadmap Items

Do not quote roadmap items as committed delivery dates to customers or prospects. Mark all Coming Soon items as "planned, subject to change." When in doubt, say "I'll confirm with the Apps team."

Application Framework Roadmap

  • Centralized upgrades via PAF — customers receive IFP improvements without manual rebuilds
  • Order Management team takes over Application Delivery process once all apps are on AAF
  • Partner enablement materials updated with each major release
  • Generation issue fixes (dependent dropdowns, line item codes) targeted in future PAF releases
